ShadeBook

Formula Notebook for Hair Colorists

About

ShadeBook is a simple, private notebook for independent hairstylists and colorists. Save client profiles, log visit formulas, attach photos, and find any client's last color in seconds.

Privacy

ShadeBook stores all data locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no cloud sync, and no analytics.

  • No data leaves your device
  • No accounts or sign-ups
  • No third-party SDKs or tracking
  • Camera access is only used when you choose to take a photo

FAQ

Will I lose my data if I delete the app?
Yes — since all data is stored on your device, deleting the app removes your data. Use the Export Client Book feature in Settings to save a PDF backup first.

Does it work on iPad?
Yes. ShadeBook works on both iPhone and iPad.
